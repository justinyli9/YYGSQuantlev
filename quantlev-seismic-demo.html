Displacement values are illustrative, scaled to read like a console rather than exact field measurements. The conventional building uses a single-degree-of-freedom model with a 1.0 Hz natural frequency and light damping, so it amplifies sharply at resonance. QuantLev is modeled as a near-frictionless isolation layer, so the ground slides beneath it while the structure stays close to fixed in space. Cooling failure removes the levitation gap and the building behaves like the conventional one.
